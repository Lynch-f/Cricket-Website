ABOUT US

Cricket Instinct is a New Zealand based Coaching, Blog and Media Platform.

We are passionate about Cricket and our role is to motivate other aspiring cricketers to be the best player they can be.

We also place significant emphasis on getting more females involved in Cricket. Womens cricket is growing in popularity. We encourage all girls interested to get involved as now is the best time to be a female in cricket!
